Leiden's Routeguesser

In this game, you are given a start and an end point (green and blue, respectively). The objective is to come up with the most optimal path possible between these points. Some paths will be blocked (marked red) depending on the difficulty chosen.

In order to make the route, you can click on the white dots shown on the map to expand the route from the current position to that point. Then, new white points will be shown allowing you to keep making your path.

After each round, you will see the actual optimal path and how well you did. Then, you can start a new round. After finishing all rounds, you will be able to view your overall performance.
